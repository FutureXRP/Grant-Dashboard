````markdown id="v7q3md"
# Section 18 — Partnerships, Coalitions, Community Engagement & Collective Impact

> **Purpose**
>
> No single organization can solve complex community challenges alone.
>
> The strongest grant applications rarely describe a single nonprofit working in isolation. Instead, they demonstrate coordinated efforts among healthcare providers, schools, nonprofits, businesses, government agencies, faith communities, and community members.
>
> The purpose of this section is to establish a comprehensive partnership strategy that enables SquareOne Compassion to build high-impact collaborations, strengthen community trust, expand organizational capacity, and increase competitiveness for local, state, federal, and private funding opportunities.
````

````markdown id="y4b8ql"
# Section 20 — Emergency Management, Crisis Response, Business Continuity & Organizational Resilience

> **Purpose**
>
> Every organization will eventually experience disruption.
>
> The defining characteristic of resilient organizations is not whether emergencies occur—it is how effectively they prepare, respond, recover, and learn.
>
> SquareOne Compassion serves families who may depend upon its healthcare, educational, wellness, and community services during periods of crisis. Maintaining operational continuity is therefore a mission responsibility as well as an organizational obligation.
>
> The purpose of this section is to establish a comprehensive emergency management and business continuity framework that protects life, safeguards organizational assets, supports informed decision-making, and enables the continuation of critical services whenever reasonably possible.

---

# Organizational Resilience Philosophy

The primary priorities during any emergency are:

1. Protect life and safety.
2. Stabilize the situation.
3. Preserve critical operations.
4. Protect organizational assets.
5. Restore services.
6. Learn from the event.

Every emergency should conclude with a structured review that improves future preparedness.

---

# Four Phases of Emergency Management

SquareOne should organize emergency planning around four continuous phases.

```text

↓

↓
````
